Our Story

⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀RBC is a prestigious institution in the field of neuroscience and scientific social studies, established four decades ago from the merger of two major companies, Synergy Inc. and Ethno Insights, originating from the Holloway and Callahan families. This union was founded in a unique context, marked by the marital union between the heirs of the respective families, consolidating not only a family bond but also a strategic partnership in the professional sphere. With a four-decade legacy, the company is recognized for its excellence in research and development, making significant contributions to advances in neuroscience and the application of social studies. The RBC remains committed to leadership in its field, driving new technologies and solutions for contemporary challenges in mental health and social well-being.


Our Values

⠀⠀⠀⠀The mission of RBC is to lead by example in the research and application of neuroscientific and social studies, constantly aiming to contribute to the well-being and progress of society. Our vision is to shape a future where knowledge and understanding of the brain and human behavior promote a healthier and more harmonious world. Our core values include integrity, excellence, and social responsibility, reflecting our commitment to conducting high-quality studies that inspire trust and foster positive change in the global community.
